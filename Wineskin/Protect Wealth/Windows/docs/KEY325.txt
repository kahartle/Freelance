1.  The principal person who is granting this Springing Durable Power of Attorney.
In this case the wife.  This is the person who is creating the springing durable power of attorney.
2.  Any other name by which the pricipal person is known, if any.

3.  The city where the principal person resides.

4.  The state where the principal person resides.  

5.  The name of the principal person's agent.
This is the person to whom the principal person is granting power of attorney.
6.  The city where the agent resides.

7.  The state where the agent resides.  

8.  The name of the first witness.
Assure that the witness signs the document.
9.  The name of the second witness.
Assure that the witness signs the document.
