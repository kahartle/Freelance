Key text 2_9
1.  The name of the Lessor.
Enter the name of the person or entity who owns the equipment.
2.  The name of the Lessee.
Enter the name of the person or entity who is leasing the equipment from the Lessor.
3.  The term of the Lease Agreement.
Enter the number of months the Lease Agreement will be in effect.
4.  The amount of the lease payments.
Enter the dollar amount (without the $ sign) of the monthly lease payment.
5.  The state whose laws will govern the Lease Agreement.
This is typically the state where the Lessor resides or maintains its principal place of business.
6.  The equipment Lessor is leasing to Lessee under this Lease Agreement.
Separate specific items of equipment with commas.
